Our Story

Built on Integrity. Grown on Results.

All Door Supply was founded over 35 years ago with a simple mission: provide contractors, builders and property managers with the right Division 8 products, delivered on time, backed by people who know what they're talking about.

That mission hasn't changed. As a family-owned and operated business, we've built our reputation one project at a time — earning the trust of some of Southern Ontario's most respected builders and contractors.

From our showroom and warehouse in Burlington, we service projects from small residential renovations to large commercial and institutional builds. Whether it's standard hollow metal or a completely custom fabrication, we deliver.
